## HealthyU

### App Description
A mobile database and tracker that allows users to follow the specific diet they want (intermittent fasting, keto, paleo, etc.). It will provide meal recipes, restaurant recommendations in the area and lists of snacks/ingredients they can consume. It will also let users track their macros and calories while theyâ€™re on the diet and provide recommendations on how they should continue/change depending on their goals for the diet.
